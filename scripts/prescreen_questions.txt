Prescreen Questions:

1. What is the main goal you’re trying to achieve right now? (1–2 sentences)
2. What’s your approximate monthly budget for solving this problem?
3. How soon are you looking to start? (ASAP, 30 days, 90 days, Just researching)

Use these in:
- Email (with a link to a form or a reply)
- SMS (one at a time)
- DMs (one at a time)

Always store answers into a sheet or CRM.
