# Lead Excel/CSV Format

Your Excel or CSV file should have headers like:

- company
- website
- first_name
- last_name
- role
- linkedin_url
- instagram_handle
- phone
- source (how you found them)
- notes (any additional context)

Example row:

Doe Consulting | Doeconsulting.com | john | doe | Founder |https://linkedin.com/in/johndoe  | @doeconsulting | +15555555555 | Clutch | Saw their case study

Upload this file to:
- Google Drive (then connect via Google Sheets / Google Drive in Make.com), or
- OneDrive / Dropbox (depending on which Make.com modules you prefer), or
- Convert it to Google Sheets first and use “Google Sheets: Watch rows / Get rows”
]
